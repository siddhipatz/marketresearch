Compact construction equipment is small in size, light in weight, highly efficient, and capable of performing multiple tasks as compared to heavy equipment.

Compact construction equipment, such as mini excavators and compact wheel loaders, delivers substantial benefits on a wide variety of projects. Typically ranging from one to six tonnes, mini excavators are lightweight, easy to transport and user-friendly. Ideal for confined areas, they can be used on utility, foundation work, landscaping and indoor demolition or construction projects.
